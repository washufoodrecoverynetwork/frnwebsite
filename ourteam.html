Our Team

OUR OPERATIONS ARE CARRIED OUT BY A GROUP OF CAPABLE LEADERS DEDICATED TO OUR MISSION.

The Shift Captains are a group of WashU students dedicated to service and making a difference in our community. They lead shifts with the responsibility, integrity, and dependability expected of truly great leaders. Out Leadership Team is composed of Shift Captains who go above and beyond to make CK x FRN WashU the best it can be, accepting higher responsibilities and directing the rest of the Shift Captains with new ideas and proven standards of quality.
